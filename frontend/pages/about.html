About AeroSense

AeroSense is a state-of-the-art air pollution detection system designed to deliver hyper-local, real-time air quality metrics globally. Our mission is to keep communities informed and protected by visualizing complex environmental feeds clearly and intuitively.


Our system integrates with high-frequency global environmental API feeds to retrieve critical parameters:


  • 🌫️ Particulate Matter (PM2.5 & PM10) — Ultrafine particles capable of entering the respiratory system.
  • 🚗 Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂) — Highly reactive gaseous pollutants primarily from vehicle emission systems.
  • ☀️ Ozone (O₃) — Ground-level atmospheric ozone formed via solar photochemical reactions.
